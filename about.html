About Me

A passionate Computer Science student combining technical expertise with design thinking to create meaningful digital experiences.

Hello! I'm a passionate developer and designer 👋

As a 2nd year Computer Science student at Heriot-Watt University, I've discovered my passion lies at the intersection of technology and user experience. I believe great software isn't just about clean code—it's about understanding users and creating solutions that genuinely improve their lives.

My dual focus on Frontend Development and UX Design allows me to bridge the gap between beautiful designs and functional implementations. I enjoy tackling complex problems and turning them into elegant, accessible solutions that users love to interact with.

Currently, I'm working on several exciting projects including a multi-user budget manager, team collaboration dashboard, and fitness tracking application. Each project challenges me to think both as a developer and a designer, ensuring the final product is both technically sound and user-centered.

What drives me 🚀

I'm fascinated by the psychology behind user interactions and how small design decisions can significantly impact user behavior. Whether it's optimizing a button placement for better accessibility or implementing smooth animations that provide meaningful feedback, I love the details that make digital experiences feel magical.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring the latest web technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sketching out ideas for my next creative venture. I'm particularly interested in the emerging trends in web accessibility and inclusive design.
